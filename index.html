About Us

Xwards is an Ad-Tech company that creates an interactive user experience for taxi cab commuters.

We understand the length to which brands go to popularize their product, and how difficult it is to grab the attention of the audience. We do just that for them by optimizing the points of wait through our network of dynamic screens.
